Local dev is driven by PowerShell scripts at repo root: `setup_and_run.ps1`, `setup_and_start.ps1`, `start_servers.ps1`, `restart_servers.ps1`, `check_servers.ps1`, `migrate_frontend.ps1`. The backend database can be bootstrapped standalone via `backend/create_tables.py` or `backend/init_database.ps1`. Production-like environment is launched with `docker compose up --build` which builds both `backend/Dockerfile`s and the frontend Dockerfile and wires Postgres/Redis/Celery together.
